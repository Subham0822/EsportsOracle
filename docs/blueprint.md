# **App Name**: Esports Oracle

## Core Features:

- Valorant Single Team Prediction: Predict win probability for a single Valorant team based on team statistics using a machine learning model.
- Valorant Team vs Team Prediction: Compare two Valorant teams head-to-head and predict the match winner. Visualize team statistics with a radar chart.
- CS:GO Team vs Team Prediction: Compare two CS:GO teams and predict the match winner. Visualize team economy and performance stats using a radar chart.
- PUBG Player Placement Prediction: Predict a PUBG player's win placement percentile based on match statistics.
- League of Legends Match Prediction: Predict whether Team 1 will win or lose a League of Legends match based on objective stats for both teams.
- Team Name Generator: Using generative AI, provide team names. The model is given the team's style as a tool, and reasons about whether to incorporate the style or not in the output. This feature should be available on team vs team prediction pages (Valorant and CS:GO) where users can generate creative team names based on a style description.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo #4B0082 to evoke a sense of mystery and expertise in gaming predictions
- Background color: Dark gray #1A1A1A to enhance the visibility of content and provide a modern gaming aesthetic
- Accent color: Electric violet #8F00FF to highlight key interactive elements such as buttons and progress bars
- Body and headline font: Inter, a sans-serif font, for a modern, readable, and neutral aesthetic
- Use emojis and icons that relate to each game, or general game-related iconography such as controllers or esports themes
- A responsive layout adapting to different screen sizes, featuring clear sections for input, output, and visualizations.
- Subtle loading animations during prediction calculations to improve user experience.
